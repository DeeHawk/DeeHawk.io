Build-My-Battlestation

CS 410 Project Coordinator: Zeed Jarrah Group Member(s): Dilruba Hawk & Zeed Jarrah

A favorite pastime of many scientists, engineers, IT professionals and content creators is building one’s own computer. These computers sometimes referred to as “Battlestations”, represent a significant time and monetary investment. There are many types of components to select from and a person new to the hobby can often feel confused on what parts to buy. Therecommender system that aggregates user data from veteran users and recommend complete PC builds that will help guide a new PC enthusiast. Our tool will recommend a list of PC components to purchased based on a certain criteria provided by the user.
